Real Estate Law

The real estate sector involves significant investments and risks, requiring meticulous legal oversight. Our firm provides comprehensive services in property sales and purchases, title deed transactions, condominium law, mortgages, and usufruct rights.

We offer expertise in drafting and implementing contracts between contractors and landowners, particularly for large-scale construction projects. In real estate development, we proactively identify legal risks and take necessary steps to protect investors’ interests.

Additionally, we provide effective representation in property disputes, dissolution of partnerships, and title deed annulment and registration cases. Our goal is to ensure that real estate transactions are completed securely and smoothly, safeguarding our clients’ investments.
